About Us

Vidya Darshan Coaching Centre is dedicated to nurturing young minds by offering quality coaching focused on school subjects and competitive exam preparation for students of Bihar Board and CBSE, from class 6th through 12th. We combine experienced faculty, customized learning plans, and a supportive environment for holistic success.

Courses We Offer

  • Bihar Board Classes 6th to 12th – Science, Math, Social Studies, English, Hindi
  • CBSE Board Classes 6th to 12th – Full syllabus coaching
  • Competitive Exam Preparation: NTSE, NDA, JEE, NEET, and other state & national level exams
  • Special Weekend Crash Courses for Exam Boosters
